About PolicyLens India

Our Mission

PolicyLens India is a neutral, citizen-first policy intelligence platform that makes government policies and schemes accessible to every Indian citizen. We believe that understanding government benefits should not be a privilege of the few.

What We Do

We simplify complex government policies and schemes into easy-to-understand language, help you discover which benefits you qualify for, and provide step-by-step guidance on how to avail them.

  • Explain policies in simple language
  • Show pros & cons transparently
  • Tell you which policies/schemes apply to you
  • Explain how to avail benefits step-by-step
Our Commitment

We are committed to neutrality, transparency, and accuracy. Our platform does not provide legal advice but serves as an awareness tool to help citizens understand available government schemes.

Legal Disclaimer

This platform does not provide legal advice. Information is for awareness purposes only. Please verify all details on official government portals before making decisions. We are not responsible for any consequences of using this information.

For the most accurate and up-to-date information, always refer to official government websites and consult with appropriate authorities.
